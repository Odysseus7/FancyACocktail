About us

Stroll through an unmarked side door at the front of Latin restaurant la Habañero and you'll end up in our bar Cocktail House. Cocktails are served only after you're seated. The Cocktail House attaches great importance to small groups, for an extraordinary expierence. Aside from our tuxedoed bartenders, a rotating list of impressive DJ's, we have conjured up this website. Because in times of isolation, a cocktail is all you need.
